I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Technology at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, advised by Prof. Chuan Shi and currently conducting research under the guidance of Enmao Diao. I completed my undergraduate study in Computer Science and Technology at BUPT in 2025. My research broadly lies in machine learning, with current interests in graph machine learning, transformers, generative modeling, and graph foundation models.
